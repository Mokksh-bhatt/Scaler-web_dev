Veg Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Butter
  • Cucumber slices
  • Tomato slices
  • Potato slices
  • Green chutney
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Spread butter and green chutney on slices of bread.
  2. Place cucumber, tomato, and potato slices on one slice.
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Cover with another slice of bread.
  5. Cut & serve your sandwich fresh!
